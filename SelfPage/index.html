ABOUT ME



I have a dream that, just like those in scinece fictions, robots can liberate productivity and collaborate with human beings, which can make our world greater and makes the life happier. So, what interests me most is those researches on robotics which can contribute to and collaborate with human beings. For recently, although robots like NAO, ASIMO, PR2 etc. were able to do much amazing works, it is really difficult for robots to parter with people in unstructured enviornment or even having safe problem in structured enviornment. So, maybe the key to a more intelligent agent is researches on AI like embodied intelligence.


I major in automatic control, but also did a lot of works on mechanical engineering and programming. I love pondering on problems, also i am highly self-motivated and efficient in independent learning.


I feel lucky and grateful that i participated in the Student Union, Club of Volley Ball and the Werstern Orchastra in my college life. I am grateful for the accompany of the music and those friends i met in schoole team and Student Union. Many Thanks!
