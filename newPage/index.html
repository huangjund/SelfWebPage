Hello, I am Junda Huang

My Research interests lie on Optimization based Control and Artificial Intelligence with the focuses on Robotics.

I am now a second year PhD student in Statistics at University of California, Los Angeles. Before that I received M.S. in Computer Science at University of Southern California, and B.Eng in Software Engineering at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China

I work as a GSR (Graduate Student Researcher) at Center for Vision Cognition Learning and Autonomy (VCLA) at University of California, Los Angeles(UCLA), advised by Dr. XiaoPing Chen and Dr. Ye Zhao.
